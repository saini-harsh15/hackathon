About the UHI Effect

The Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect occurs when urban areas experience significantly higher temperatures than surrounding rural areas. This is due to the heat-absorbing materials used in buildings and infrastructure. Mitigating the UHI effect is essential to reduce energy consumption, improve public health, and support biodiversity.

Our Solution

Our platform leverages AI and Machine Learning to predict heat hotspots and identify optimal locations for green spaces, such as parks, trees, and vegetation. This approach helps urban planners make informed decisions to mitigate the UHI effect.
